U2Xperience

The U2Xperience is Southern California's newest U2 tribute band and is truly one of the best sounding and most accurate U2 tribute bands in the world!

​

Bono, The Edge, Larry Mullen, Jr. and Adam Clayton are U2, the world’s greatest rock band. U2X delivers spectacular live recreations of U2 concerts from all eras and tours by an ensemble of four veteran U2 tribute performers who collectively have played close to 1,000 tribute shows since 2006. Long-time U2 fans themselves, each member of U2X is personally committed to recreating the most authentic, memorable U2 experience for you, your friends, guests, customers and employees.

Like U2 itself, our performances require an incredible amount of musicianship, passion, spontaneity, interaction with the audience and mastery of modern technical tools. What you'll see and hear is performed live exactly as U2 themselves would do it.

The best of breed musicians in U2X have entertained tens of thousands of U2 fans locally, nationally and internationally. Let us amaze “you too” with incredible vocals, echo-drenched guitars, thunderous drums and driving bass. You won't believe your ears or your eyes!
